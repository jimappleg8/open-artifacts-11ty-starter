{% pageHeader "How to Contribute", "Got an idea about how to make this Open Artifacts project better? We welcome contributions from the community!" %}

The whole point of an Open Artifacts project is to take a good idea and make it better with the help of the community. You may wonder if you qualify as a member of the community. The answer is yes! If you are reading this, you are part of the community.

There are <strong>many ways to contribute</strong> to this project, including:

- Spreading the word about the project
- [Looking at the roadmap](/project/oa-roadmap/) and choosing some bit of work that's yours to do.
- Using this Open Artifacts project by adapting it to your own needs and sharing your improvements.
